General

What is Formment? What does it do?

Formment is a versatile software platform designed to ensure brand consistency and deliver the intended audience reaction through advanced slide analysis. Tailored for professionals in sectors like investment banking, it streamlines workflows and enhances decision-making with customizable dashboards and analytics.

How does Formment ensure the security and confidentiality of our data?

Formment ensures the security and confidentiality of your data by hosting all operations locally on your computer, ensuring that your information never leaves your local environment. This approach eliminates external data breaches and maintains complete control over your sensitive information.

How can Formment demonstrate a return on investment for your active deals?

Formment demonstrates a return on investment for active deals by streamlining operations, reducing manual labor through automation, enhancing decision-making with real-time data analytics, and minimizing errors, which collectively lead to cost savings and improved operational efficiency. These improvements directly contribute to higher productivity and increased profitability, clearly showcasing a tangible ROI.

What kind of training and support does Formment offer to new users?

Formment offers comprehensive training and support to new users through a variety of video demos available on our platform. These videos cover everything from basic setup and navigation to advanced features and troubleshooting, providing a self-paced, visual learning experience. You can find all our training materials here to help you maximize the use of the platform effectively.

Yes, Formment provides a trial period so you can explore the platform's features and assess its fit for your deal making needs before making a decision.
